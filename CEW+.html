Center for the Education of Women + (CEW+)

At the Center for the Education of Women + (CEW+), wherever you are from, we're here to empower all University of Michigan students, especially those with unique or marginalized identities.

Scholarships and Fundings

  • CEW+ Scholarships: Designed for students who have overcome obstacles, including women and underserved students returning to school later in life.
  • Emergency Funding: When unexpected financial challenges arise, we offer emergency grants to help you stay focused on your education.

Counseling

We are committed to helping UM students through personalized counseling and career coaching. Whether you're facing academic challenges, navigating personal situations, balancing school and life, or seeking career-related advise, schedule an appointment, and we're here to listen and help you explore options, develop skills, and create strategies to achieve your goals.

Three ways to schedule an appointment

Events & Workshops

  • Skill-Building Workshops, Speaker Events and Lectures: From leadership development to learning to understand financial documents, our workshops provide the tools you need to succeed both in and outside of the classroom.
  • Research: If you are interested in advocating for policies that support student success, we also conduct research and welcome you to join us.

CEW+ Advocacy Initiatives

We work to challenge inequities and influence policies that make a real difference for students and the broader community. We advocate for the inclusion and equity of all students, with a focus on addressing the needs of those who are often overlooked, such as:

  • Women and Gender Minorities
  • Older students, caregivers, and veterans
  • Underserved Populations, including first-generation students, students of color, LGBTQ+ students, and students with disabilities
  • Women of Color Task Force (WCTF): A long-standing program that promotes the professional development and leadership of women of color at UM through workshops, networking, and community-building events.
