Center for the Education of Women + (CEW+)

At the Center for the Education of Women + (CEW+), wherever you are from, we're here to empower all University of Michigan students, especially those with unique or marginalized identities.

Scholarships and Fundings


  • First-Generation Center
    Designed for students who have overcome obstacles, including women and underserved students returning to school later in life.
  • Emergency Funding
    When unexpected financial challenges arise, we offer emergency grants to help you stay focused on your education.

Counseling


We are committed to helping UM students through personalized counseling and career coaching. Whether you're facing academic challenges, navigating personal situations, balancing school and life, or seeking career-related advise, schedule an appointment, and we're here to listen and help you explore options, develop skills, and create strategies to achieve your goals.

Events & Workshops


  • Skill-Building Workshops, Speaker Events and Lectures
    From leadership development to learning to understand financial documents, our workshops provide the tools you need to succeed both in and outside of the classroom.
  • Research
    If you are interested in advocating for policies that support student success, we also conduct research and welcome you to join us.

CEW+ Advocacy Initiatives


  • Underserved Populations
    Initiatives for women and gender minorities, older students, caregivers, and veterans, first-generation students, students of color, LGBTQ+ students, and students with disabilities, and so forth.
  • Women of Color Task Force (WCTF)
    Women of Color Task Force is a long-standing program that promotes the professional development and leadership of women of color at UM through workshops, networking, and community-building events.
