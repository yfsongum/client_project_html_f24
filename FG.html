First-Generation Student Center

Being the first in your family to attend college comes with its own set of challenges and opportunities, and we're here to provide the resources and support you need to thrive in the University of Michigan.

First-Gen Gateway

Whether you're looking for academic support, community connections, or just a place to ask questions, the Gateway is your first stop to get connected to resources for first-generation students.

  • Location: Office of Academic Multicultural Initiatives on the 3rd floor of the Student Activities Building at 515 E. Jefferson St.
  • Leadership Team from four key offices on campus:
    • Comprehensive Studies Program (CSP)
    • Multi-Ethnic Student Affairs (MESA)
    • Office of Academic Multicultural Initiatives (OAMI)
    • Office of New Student Programs (ONSP)

Paying for School

We understand that financial planning can be one of the most stressful parts of the college experience, especially for first-generation students. Navigate our website for everything you need to know about managing finances at UM.

  • Understanding Your Financial Aid Package: financial aid, loans, and scholarships
  • Budgeting Tools: learn how to create a budget that works for your needs, from managing daily expenses to planning for the future.
  • Emergency Funds
