International Center

At the University of Michigan International Center (IC), we're here to support international students in every step of their journey at the University of Michigan. Whether you're new to campus or looking for ongoing support, we offer a wide range of services, resources, and opportunities that are tailored just for you.

Immigration and Visa


  • F-1 and J-1 visas
    We provide guidance for the initial application and maintaining status of your student visas.
  • Work Authorization
    We guide you through the process of applying for OPT (Optional Practical Training) or CPT (Curricular Practical Training) authorizations so you can gain valuable work experience in the U.S.
  • Travel Support
    Unsure about travel restrictions or documentation requirements? We help ensure your travels are smooth and stress-free.

Cultural Support


  • International Student Orientation
    A fun and informative way to get familiar with campus life and meet other students.
  • Cross-cultural Programs
    Events and activities to help you meet students all over the world and explore new cultures.
  • Counseling and Wellness Resources
    We partner with campus health services (i.e., CAPS) to help connect with you to counselors who understand the unique challenges international students face.

Academic and Career Guidance


  • Academic Workshops
    Workshops focused on study strategies, classroom expectations, and so forth.
  • Career Development
    From resume building to interview prep, we'll help you navigate career fairs and job searches, both in the U.S. and abroad.
  • Academic Advising
    You can always seek for help from advisors regarding academic, personal, and immigration-related questions.
