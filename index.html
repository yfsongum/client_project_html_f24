Academic Success

Maximize your academic potential with resources designed to guide you through every step of your journey at UMSI. From personalized advising to study spaces and tutoring services, find everything you need to stay on top of your coursework and achieve your academic goals.

  • MSI Academic Advising Website
    • Important Dates (Fall 2024)
      Stay informed about key deadlines such as course registration, drop/add periods, and university holidays to keep your academic journey on track.
    • Academic Advising
      Access one-on-one advising sessions to receive insights into course requirements, degree progress, and academic strategies.
    • Help! Who do I contact?
      Discover the right contacts for various student services, ensuring you reach the correct advisor or department for your specific needs.
    • Tutoring Site
      Connect with tutoring resources that can provide you with extra support in challenging courses and subjects.
  • Study spaces on Campus
    Explore a variety of study spaces across campus, perfect for focused work or group study sessions.
  • U-M Library
    Access a vast array of academic resources, including research databases, journals, and study materials.

School-life Balance

Achieve a fulfilling balance between your studies and personal life with our curated support services. Explore resources like CEW+, the International Center, and student organizations that help you thrive both inside and outside the classroom.

  • Center for the Education of Women+
    Get access to resources that support the personal, academic, and professional goals of women and underserved students.
  • International Center
    The go-to place for international students to find information on visas, cultural adjustment, and international student programming.
  • First-Generation Center
    Resources and support tailored to the unique needs of first-generation college students at U-M.
  • Basic Needs
    Learn about the services that address essential needs like food, housing, and financial stability.
  • Recreational Sports
    Find opportunities to engage in physical activity through intramural sports, fitness classes, and recreational facilities.
  • Student Organizations
    Connect with student-led groups and organizations to build community and explore your interests outside the classroom.

Resources in times of crisis

Find the support you need during challenging times with our range of crisis resources. Access services like the food pantry, counseling, and wellness programs to help you overcome obstacles and maintain your well-being.

  • Emergency & Supportive Contacts
    Call for support and help if you are in a difficult time.
  • Food Pantry
    Access food support services for students experiencing food insecurity or needing immediate assistance.
  • Student Support Services
    Find comprehensive support for academic, personal, and social challenges, ensuring students succeed during difficult times.
    • English Language Institute (ELI)
      Improve your English language skills with courses and workshops tailored for non-native speakers.
    • Sweetland Writing Center
      Receive individualized writing support, from brainstorming ideas to refining your final draft.
    • Services for Students with Disabilities (SSD)
      Learn about the accommodations and support available for students with disabilities to ensure equitable access to all campus resources.
  • Counceling & Psychological Services
    Get support for mental health challenges through counseling, workshops, and therapy sessions.
    • UMSI Embedded CAPS Counselor
      A dedicated counselor is available for UMSI students, providing tailored mental health support within the school.
    • Peer Counseling
      Connect with trained student counselors who can offer a listening ear and peer support during challenging times.
    • Wolverine Wellness
      Access wellness programs and resources that promote holistic well-being, from sleep and nutrition to stress management.
  • University Health Services (UHS)
    Access a range of healthcare services, from routine medical care to mental health support, right on campus.
